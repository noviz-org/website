Speaking Radar

What is the intention of this App?

You point the phone in a direction and double tap to start.
The App then tells you all the relevant places in that specific direction.

Many blind people lack a sense of oversight or wonder about their surroundings. This App can help to align your mental map with surroundings.

The intention of this App is not to be used for navigation, but to provide useful and interesting information about your environment, while functioning intuitively like a body part.

The App works as follows:

The App gets your GPS location and compass data from your phone. The App then makes a request to the Google Places database. Finally, the App converts the relevant information into speech and tells you what the interesting places in that direction are.
